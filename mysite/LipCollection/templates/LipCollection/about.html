About

I have been collecting lip items since my junior year of high school (2015). It first started with an innocent Orange-Mango Blast Blistex lip balm, where I loved the scent and couldn't resist applying it constantly. After that, I bought the unique and fun Chapstick Strawberry-Banana Smoothie Mixstick, with two ends and amazing flavors. Then came Softlips watermelon, with its slim tube and fantastic scent, and the crisp green apple Chapstick followed.
After that, I started noticing the flavorful tubes of wax at all the stores around me. On my summer trip to Chicago, I added every flavor of the Revlon Kiss lip balms to my collection. I collected a ton of new Chapstick flavors, also adding more Softlips and Blistex. I fell in love with the EOS tube formula and flavors. I also loved the fancy Pacifica shimmery tinted lip balm, that also had a great scent.
I'm now so addicted to lip products, that I have a monthly budget to how many I can add to my collection. I currently almost have 800 lip balm, 90 lip gloss, and 50 lip scrubs. I have them proudly on display, like trophies or a collection of silver spoons. I also always have four different flavors of lip balm in my pocket at a time, because I need to reapply lip balm to keep my lips moisturized 24/7. It's definitely not at world records yet, considering the record for lip balm is around 5000. However, I definitely am not planning on stopping buying lip balm, so I might hit a world record someday!
